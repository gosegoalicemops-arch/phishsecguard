SECURITY ALERT: You've Been Phished

This is a simulated phishing exercise by PhishGuard

What Just Happened?

You just interacted with a simulated phishing email as part of a security awareness exercise. In a real attack, this could have compromised your personal information, financial data, or company systems.

IMPORTANT: This was only a test, but it demonstrates a vulnerability that real attackers could exploit.

Immediate Actions You Should Take

  • If you entered any credentials, change those passwords immediately
  • Enable multi-factor authentication on all important accounts
  • Report suspicious emails to your IT security team
  • Be extra vigilant for follow-up phishing attempts

How to Spot Phishing Attempts

Phishing emails often contain these red flags:

  • Urgent or threatening language demanding immediate action
  • Suspicious sender addresses that don't match the claimed organization
  • Generic greetings instead of your name
  • Links that don't match the displayed text when you hover over them
  • Requests for sensitive information like passwords or credit card details
  • Poor grammar, spelling mistakes, or unusual formatting

What is PhishGuard?

PhishGuard is a security awareness platform that helps organizations train employees to recognize and avoid phishing attacks. By simulating real-world phishing scenarios, we help build your cyber defense skills.

This exercise was designed to create awareness without causing actual harm. Your participation helps strengthen our collective security posture.
